PORTFOLIO ABOUT

The assignment was to work in a team of two and make a “casual game” where the user must hit an annoying spinning fly to stop I from buzzing around. The fly should stay within the browser window and move randomly all over it while buzzing annoyingly until clicked. We decided to make our own version and make the game a little more colourful by using a cat in a living room instead of a fly on a white background. My main role in this project was to add the audio and work on the javascript.
